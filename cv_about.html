Cherokee Vineyard

About Us

Cherokee Vineyard is a micro-vineyard located in the hills of Marietta, SC. We grow over 30 types of organic muscadine grape varieties. Cherokee Vineyards was established in 2019 and has grown each year.

At Cherokee Vineyard we make our own muscadine jelly. Our grapes are picked at peak freshness for the best jelly flavor. Come tour our vinyard and sample our muscadine jelly!

Contact us to tour our vineyard, learn about our muscadine varieties, and taste the finest muscadine jelly in the upstate. Town Magazine calls Cherokee Vineyard the sweetest stop on your way to the mountains.
